About Me

Get to know me!

As a proud, first-generation daughter of immigrants, my academic journey has been shaped by the resilience and sacrifices of my family. I am deeply curious about the intersections between economics and law, exploring how these fields can work together to address societal challenges and promote equity. This curiosity fuels my desire to create meaningful change, whether through advocacy, community engagement, or research. Outside of my busy school schedule, I love to hike, read, and curate playlists on Spotify. Looking forward to connecting with you!
