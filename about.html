About Me

Welcome to my site! I'm a student fascinated by the intersections between economics and law, and how these fields can work together to address challenges and promote equity. I explore these ideas through research, advocacy, and community engagement, always looking for new opportunities to learn. Outside of school, I love spending time enjoying La Jolla weather, getting lost in a good book, and curating playlists on Spotify. I'm looking forward to connecting with you!
