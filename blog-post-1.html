CozyLib: A Personal Project

12/15/25
Personal

Welcome to my first post! This is mainly to confirm the formatting, and test out this part of my site.

Background

I enjoy reading, both physical books and on my Kindle. While putting together my reading list for winter break, I thought it would be fun to create a cozy little game that incorporated my books. I love Goodreads, but I also want to build a new skill and dip my toes into amateur game development. Thus, CozyLib was born!

This project is still in its very early stages of development, and I intend to keep it as a side project. Here's a mini-breakdown of how I imagine it to work.

Design

Growing up, I loved playing Zelda on my Nintendo DS. The nostalgic pixel art style returned in a love for Stardew Valley, which serves as the main inspiration as the style for this game.

Conclusion
